Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2025

1. The Purpose of This Project

This website is a non-commercial, educational portfolio project created by M. Murat Ardag . Its sole purpose is to demonstrate the types of data that can be collected from a visitor's browser during a typical web session. The goal is to raise awareness about digital privacy and client-side data logging in a transparent way.

2. What Information We Collect

To fulfill its educational purpose, this site logs the following information associated with your visit:

  • Initial Visit Data: Your browser's user agent, the referring URL (how you got here), and your screen dimensions.
  • Navigational Data: The pages you visit on this website.
  • Interaction Data: All mouse movements, clicks, and scrolls you make on the page.
  • Input Data: Any text you type into form fields on this site, excluding the password field.
  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we will store the email address you provide.

This data is linked to a temporary session ID, which is then associated with your user account if you choose to sign up.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used for one reason only: to display it back to you. By clicking the "Show me what the website logs about me" button, you can see the data that has been recorded during your session. This information is not used for marketing, sold to third parties, or analyzed for any purpose other than this demonstration.

4. Data Storage and Retention

All collected data is stored securely in a Google Firebase (Firestore) database. Access to this data is restricted, and it is only retrieved to be shown to you, the user who generated it. To further protect privacy and minimize data retention, all logged event data is permanently deleted every 30 days.

5. Your Consent and Control

By using this website, you consent to the data logging activities described in this policy for the educational purposes outlined. You have full control to view the data logged during your session at any time.
